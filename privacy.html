1. Introduction

DevBox (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our mobile application DevBox.

2. Information We Collect

Since DevBox does not require user registration, we do not collect personal information directly. However, we may automatically collect non-personal information through third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: usage data, device type, OS version, app interactions.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: crash reports and technical diagnostics.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): device tokens for delivering notifications.
  • Firebase Realtime Database: if used for app functionality, may store temporary app data.

We do not send data to our own servers.

3. How We Use the Information

To provide and improve DevBox features.

To analyze performance and fix technical issues.

To deliver optional push notifications.

4. Sharing of Information

With Firebase/Google as service providers.

We do not sell or share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing.

5. User Rights

As DevBox does not store personal accounts, user data is minimal and managed through Firebase.

For data-related requests (e.g., Firebase data deletion), contact us at c.steytcore@outlook.com.

6. Children’s Privacy

DevBox is intended for general use and not specifically targeted at children. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

7. Data Security

We rely on Google Firebase’s security standards, including encryption and access controls, to protect collected information.

8. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be published here with a new effective date.

9. Contact

If you have questions, contact us at: c.steytcore@outlook.com.
